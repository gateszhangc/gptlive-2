The GPT Live difference

No more waiting for a clean handoff.

Most voice systems need a clear signal that you have finished talking. GPT Live keeps processing speech while it generates a response, so a pause can stay a pause and an interruption can become part of the same exchange.

GPT Live explained

What is GPT Live, exactly?

GPT Live is OpenAI's family of voice models built for continuous, natural human-AI conversation. Unlike voice pipelines that separately transcribe speech, generate an answer, and read it aloud, GPT Live handles audio interaction as an ongoing exchange.

Its full-duplex architecture repeatedly decides whether to speak, keep listening, pause, respond to an interruption, or invoke a tool. At launch, GPT‑Live‑1 powers paid ChatGPT Voice plans, while GPT‑Live‑1 mini powers the Free experience.

Frequently asked questions

Before you start talking.

The current site is a static product preview. These answers separate the interface concept from a production voice service.

What is GPT Live?+

GPT Live is OpenAI's new generation of full-duplex voice models. It can listen and speak continuously, handle interruptions and pauses, and delegate harder work such as search or deeper reasoning.

Is this GPT Live website affiliated with OpenAI?+

No. This website and interface concept are independent and are not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by OpenAI.

Where can I use GPT Live?+

GPT Live is rolling out in ChatGPT Voice on ChatGPT.com, iOS, and Android. At launch, paid consumer plans use GPT‑Live‑1 and the Free plan uses GPT‑Live‑1 mini. Availability may vary.

Does this preview call a live AI model?+

No. The current static interface demo uses local sample text. It does not call a model, upload microphone data, or create an account.

What are the current GPT Live limitations?+

At launch, Live does not support video or screen sharing and is designed mainly for one-on-one conversation. Background noise, overlapping speech, network conditions, and microphone settings can affect what it hears.
